About Me


Now we come to the section where you get to learn about me, some of what I’ve done, what I do now, and what I want to do in the future. I grew up in the South so I’m a huge fan of anything to do with the outdoors. Hunting, fishing, diving, and running have all at some point been important to me. When I graduated high school, I decided that college wasn’t my idea of amazing and joined the Marines as an electronics technician. I served 5 years there and in 2004 I was honorably discharged. Upon my discharge I decided I should try college and realized that it still wasn’t for me. This had a lot to do with being the first person in my family to attempt a 4-year degree and no one being there to help me. I didn’t know anything about what I was doing so I performed horribly. (I would attempt to go back several times over the course of my life, and it never went the way it should have.) After this first attempt, I worked a few dead-end jobs and decided in 2007 that I was going into the Army. I served in the Army from 2007 to 2011 and was discharged again late that year. I have since attempted to go back to school unsuccessfully. I am retired currently and learning to code as a hobby and to attempt to help lower-financed non-profit organizations with development that they might otherwise be unable to afford.
